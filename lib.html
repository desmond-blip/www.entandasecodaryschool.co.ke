Entanda Secondary School Library System

Library Usage Guide

The Entanda Secondary School Library is open to all students and staff for academic purposes. Students can read, borrow, and return textbooks, revision books, and reference materials.

How to Borrow a Book:

  • Search for the book using the catalog or ask the librarian.
  • Present your student ID to borrow.
  • Borrowing is limited to 2 books for 2 weeks.

How to Return a Book:

  • Return the book on or before the due date.
  • Late returns will incur penalties.

Library Rules and Regulations:

  • Maintain silence at all times.
  • No food or drinks allowed.
  • Do not damage or write in books.
  • Use your ID when borrowing books.
