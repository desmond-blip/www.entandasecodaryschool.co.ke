Entanda Secondary School

Mr. Desmond Mabuka

As the Senior Teacher of Entanda Secondary School, I am pleased to highlight our academic direction and responsibilities.

My role includes closely tracking student performance, ensuring no learner is left behind, and driving data-based interventions. Regular academic reviews help us celebrate progress and address any challenges early.

I also oversee the smooth coordination of all subject departments. This includes supporting Heads of Departments (HODs) in curriculum implementation, lesson planning, and departmental evaluations to maintain high academic standards.

Together with the teaching team, we are committed to academic excellence, professional collaboration, and student success.

🌟 Vision

To be a center of academic excellence producing holistic, empowered and responsible citizens.

🎯 Mission

To provide quality education that inspires learners to achieve their best through discipline, integrity and innovation.
