Welcome to the Deputy Principal's Office

Entanda Secondary School

Mr. Desmond Mabuka

As the Deputy Principal of Entanda Secondary School, I take this opportunity to speak to all teachers, students, and non-teaching staff on the importance of discipline in building a thriving school environment. Discipline is the foundation of academic excellence, mutual respect, and professional integrity. Let us all commit to upholding punctuality, honesty, responsibility, and order in our daily routines.

To the students: your future depends on how you conduct yourselves today. Respect time, follow school rules, and strive to be your best.

To the staff: let’s model the discipline we expect from our learners and contribute to a positive, organized, and nurturing environment.

To our non-teaching staff: your role in supporting the school is highly valued. Let’s continue serving diligently and professionally.

🌟 Vision

To be a center of academic excellence producing holistic, empowered and responsible citizens.

🎯 Mission

To provide quality education that inspires learners to achieve their best through discipline, integrity and innovation.
